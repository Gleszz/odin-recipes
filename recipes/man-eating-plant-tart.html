Man-Eating Plant Tart

A nutritious tart made with Man-Eating Plant, rich and vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients

  • 200 ml of Scorpion and Slime broth
  • 80 g of Slime gelatin
  • 3 medium Baraselia
  • 5 to 6 medium Meeroak
  • 3 medium Betan
  • Salt to taste
  • A pinch of Pepper

Directions

  1. Lightly Steam the fruit of the plants, cut around the stem, pull it and the seed should come off easily.
  2. Remove the peels and pound them until they're soft.
  3. Cover the bottom of a frying pan with them.
  4. Mash the unripe fruit and add a little of scorpion broth with slime gelatin.
  5. Mix everything until it gets sticky.
  6. Add the rest of the scorpion broth and the chopped fruit.
  7. Give it a few good stirs until it gets smooth.
  8. Pour the mixture in the frying pan.
  9. Heat it for a while.
  10. Once the surface starts to bubble put the rest of the fruit on top.
