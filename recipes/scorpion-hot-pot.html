Scorpion Hot Pot

A delicious hot pot made with a huge scorpion, walking mushroom and other ingredients found in the dungeon. Especially good for a novice adventuring party containing 3 to 4 members

Ingredients

  • 1 Huge Scorpion
  • 1 Walking Mushroom
  • 2 Mushroom feet
  • Seaweed (arctic moss, star jelly) to taste
  • About 5 medium Invertatoes
  • As much Dried Slime as you like
  • Water to taste

Directions

  1. Take off the pincer, head, legs and tail of the huge scorpion.
  2. Cut scores along the body of the huge scorpion and take out the innards.
  3. Throw away the rump and three centimeters of the surface of the walking mushroom, but keep the legs.
  4. Wash your slime well in hot water and citrus juice.
  5. Either wipe the moisture off or knead salt into it.
  6. Let it dry off (preferably in the sun as it takes a lot of time).
  7. Cut your invertatoes, slime and seaweed in pieces and add everything in the pot.
  8. Cook everything in hot water until the scorpion meat turns red.
