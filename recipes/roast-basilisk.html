Roast Basilisk

A roast Basilisk full in fat and protein, it'll give you the energy to explore the dungeon

Ingredients

  • 1 Basilisk (tail and breast meat removed)
  • Mana herb to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Stuffing:
  • 1 Medium Antitoxin herb
  • 1 Root of good Medicinal herb
  • 1 Root of amazing Medicinal herb
  • 1 Medium Deparalysis herb
  • 1 Medium Depetrification herb

Directions

  1. Cut off the legs and the tail of the Basilisk.
  2. Scald it lightly, then pluck it.
  3. Take out the innards of the Basilisk.
  4. Rub in the spices to your taste.
  5. Let it sit overnight for the flavor to marry.
  6. Stuff the breast cavity with minced herbs.
  7. Tie up the cut.
  8. Run a skewer through the meat.
  9. Then roast it well.
